Visiting New York

As the title suggests and as an example, today I will be sharing some tips when visiting New York.

First off, I want to state that VISITING Is a blast.
I say visit because living is a way different experience.(trust me)

Now here are some of my favorite places to see when visiting New York:

  • One World Observatory
  • Statue of Liberty
  • Times Square

Here is my list of things to do on a short trip to New York:

  1. Have pizza at Joe's pizza in Times Square
  2. Stroll through Central Park and visit the Zoo!
  3. Visit during the holidays to see the Rockefeller Christmas tree lighting
  4. Take a national landmark walk and see top sites like the Empire State Building, Brooklyn Bridge, and more!
  5. Remember loved ones lost at the World Trade Center Memorial
